That first app turned into 7. Every one of them solves a real problem I've seen firsthand — in retail, contracting, pet care, reselling, and dropshipping. I build with Python/Flask, SQLite, and AI integration using OpenRouter. All apps are deployed on Railway with multi-tenant architecture and Stripe billing.

I build fast, test everything, and ship clean code. No agency, no team — just me and the tools I've built.
